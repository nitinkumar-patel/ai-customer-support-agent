# E-Commerce Refund Policy

## Overview
All refund requests must be evaluated strictly against the following rules. Agents must never approve a refund that violates any applicable rule, regardless of customer sentiment.

---

## Rule 1 — Standard Return Window
Standard-tier customers may request a refund within **30 days** of the purchase date. Requests submitted after 30 days are automatically ineligible.

## Rule 2 — Premium Customer Extended Window
Premium-tier customers enjoy an extended return window of **45 days** from the purchase date. This is the only exception to Rule 1.

## Rule 3 — No Refunds on Sale Items (Change of Mind)
Items purchased at a discounted or sale price (`is_sale_item: true`) are **not eligible for refunds due to change of mind**. The only exception is if the item arrived damaged or defective.

## Rule 4 — Electronics Must Be Unopened (Change of Mind)
For the `electronics` category, refunds due to change of mind are only accepted if the item is **unopened and in original packaging**. If the item has been opened, only defect or damage claims qualify.

## Rule 5 — Health & Consumable Products
Items in the `health` category (supplements, protein powders, vitamins) that have been opened are **not eligible for refunds** under any non-defect circumstance. Unopened health products may be returned within the standard window.

## Rule 6 — Beauty & Fragrance Products
Opened beauty and fragrance products (`beauty` category) are **not eligible for refunds** due to hygiene reasons, regardless of the reason stated. Only sealed, unopened items qualify.

## Rule 7 — Defective or Damaged Items
Any item from any category that arrives **damaged or is defective** qualifies for a full refund or replacement within **60 days** of purchase, regardless of whether it has been opened. Proof of defect (description) is sufficient; physical return may be requested separately.

## Rule 8 — Wrong Item Delivered
If the customer received a different product than ordered, they are entitled to a **full refund or re-shipment** at no cost, regardless of category or sale status. This must be resolved within 60 days of delivery.

## Rule 9 — Undelivered Orders
If an order has not yet been delivered (`delivered: false`), the customer is eligible for a **full cancellation and refund** at any time before delivery is confirmed.

## Rule 10 — Books and Media
Books, digital media, and collectibles that have been opened are **not eligible for a refund** due to change of mind. Damaged or incorrect items are covered under Rule 7 and Rule 8.

## Rule 11 — Apparel Category
Apparel items may be returned within the applicable return window provided they are **unworn, unwashed, and have original tags attached**. Items returned without tags or showing signs of use will be denied. An exception is made for wrong-size deliveries where the retailer shipped the incorrect size.

## Rule 12 — Home Appliances
Defective home appliances are covered under Rule 7. Non-defective returns are accepted only if the item is **unopened**. Opened, functioning appliances returned due to change of mind are not eligible.

## Rule 13 — Maximum Refund Frequency
A customer may not receive more than **3 refunds in any rolling 12-month period**, regardless of eligibility. Exceeding this limit results in automatic denial until the 12-month window resets.

## Rule 14 — Refund Method
Approved refunds are issued to the **original payment method only**. No cash, gift card, or store credit substitutions are permitted unless the original payment method is no longer valid.

## Rule 15 — Customer Account in Good Standing
Customers whose accounts have been flagged for fraud or policy abuse are not eligible for refunds. The agent must check account status before approving.
